Biography

I am now a postdoctoral researcher in the Computer Vision Group at Technical University of Munich (TUM) working with Prof. Daniel Cremers. I am also a research scientist at Munich Center for Machine Learning. I obtained my phd degree from TUM supervised by Prof. Uwe Stilla and Prof. Daniel Cremers, and was a visiting phd student in the Visual Geometry Group (VGG) at University of Oxford working with Dr. JoÃ£o Henriques. My research interests focus on 3D Vision, Localization and Navigation, Robotics, and Autonomous Driving.
